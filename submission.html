Submissions guidelines

Abstracts or full papers will be accepted in English and WORD（.doc）format only. Please submit your papers/abstracts via Online System.
  1. Full Paper Submission (Presentation and publication)
    • Accepted full paper will be for both presentation and publication in the conference proceedings or ESCI indexed journal Nano LIFE.
    • Papers should have Abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, References, and they should have a minimum length of 10 A4 pages. (Download Full Paper Template)
  2. Abstract Submission (Presentation only)
    • Accepted abstract will be just for oral presentation or poster presentation at the conference and will not be officially published. (Download Abstract Template)

    For more information, please contact us at ncbta@gla.ac.in .
