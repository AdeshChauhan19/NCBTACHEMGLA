Rules/Guidelines

  • Registration fee includes certificate of participation.
  • A separate publication fee have to be paid by the authors for publication of their manuscript in conference proceedings or as a journal article.
  • If multiple authors of the same paper are attending the conference, then at least one of the author (preferably paper presenter) has to pay the full registration fee, whereas, rest of the authors can pay only Rs. 500 for getting participation certificate if needed. Different registration forms are required for each author.
  • No CANCELLATION & REFUND of Registration Fee.
  • Please note that the registration fee is only for attending the conference etc. Publication fee is applicable only after acceptance of the article.
